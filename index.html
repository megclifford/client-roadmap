The Foundation
Observation & Easy Wins
▼

What's Happening

This is your starting point — not your results phase. We're learning your patterns, building tracking habits, and laying the foundation before your plan is prescribed. Think of it as the prerequisite course.

What to Expect

You may or may not see the scale move right away — and that's completely normal. Some clients see early progress just from tracking. Others need a few weeks to calibrate. Either way, this phase is working.

What We're Watching

  • Your real-life calorie baseline
  • Typical daily steps & water intake
  • Tracking consistency & habits
  • Your lifestyle patterns & schedule

Graduate to Stage 2 When…

  • Logging food 80–90% of days
  • Baseline steps & water established
  • Coach can read your maintenance calories
  • Communication rhythm is working

Weeks 4–6
The Prescription
Your Custom Plan Begins
▼

What's Happening

You've earned it. Your coach lays out your full game plan — your deficit, your workouts, your specific goals. We'll also talk through your history and get ahead of the places people typically fall off.

What to Expect

Things get more intentional here. You'll have specific targets to hit. Progress should start to become visible. This is the beginning of your 12–16 week deficit window.

What's Being Prescribed

  • Your calorie or step-based deficit
  • Workout program (introduced gradually)
  • Daily step & hydration targets
  • Your non-negotiables & habit plan

What to Master

  • Consistently hitting your nutrition targets
  • Getting to the gym & learning your workouts
  • Understanding & owning your plan

The Work
Active Fat Loss + Progressive Overload

Weeks 7–10: Momentum

You're in it. This is where consistency builds real results. We'll anticipate the most common slip point — around weeks 8–10 when the initial excitement wears off — so you push through it strong.

Weeks 10–12: Optimization

Locking in the habits that stick forever. We'll review what's genuinely changed, what still needs work, and begin the bigger-picture conversation about what comes next for you.

What May Be Added

  • Cardio (only if progress stalls & food is already low)
  • Nutrient timing around workouts
  • Light food quality coaching
  • Step increases before calorie cuts

What to Master

  • Training 3–4 days/week consistently
  • Tracking without overthinking it
  • Progressive overload in the gym
  • Managing your personal obstacles

Weeks 12–13
The Reassessment
Milestone Check-In + Goal Path Call
▼

What's Happening

Time to zoom out. Where did you start? Where are you now? Your coach reviews your full progress — physical, habit, and mindset — and maps out what comes next.

Two Paths Forward

Close to goal: Begin a 3–6 week reverse/maintenance phase, then transition to Legacy.

More work to do: This is your continued journey conversation — options, timeline, what Phase II looks like.

What We Review Together

  • Physical progress: scale, measurements, how you feel in your body
  • Habit shifts: what has genuinely changed for good vs. what's still inconsistent
  • Mindset growth: which limiting beliefs have shifted and what still needs work
  • Remaining goals and a realistic timeline to get there

Weeks 13–16
The Next Chapter
Phase II or Legacy — Your Continued Journey
▼

This Isn't Starting Over

You have all the skills now. Phase II is where we use everything you've built — and finish the job. Most clients reach their full goal in 10–12 months total. That's the process working exactly as designed.

About the Reverse Phase

After a long cut, your metabolism needs to reset. A controlled 3–6 week reverse restores energy, improves gym performance, and sets your body up to respond faster and more efficiently in your next cut.

Phase II Options

  • 4 months — Close to goal; reverse + maintenance
  • 6 months — Most common; moderate remaining work
  • 8 months — Meaningful goals still ahead
  • 12 months — Comprehensive transformation

Legacy Program

When you've reached your goal and are ready to maintain independently, Legacy gives you continued access to workouts, weekly group calls, and check-ins — your forever home in the Transform Strong community.
