The Foundation
Observation & Easy Wins
▼

What's Happening

This is your starting point — not your results phase. We're learning your patterns, getting comfortable with tracking, and building the foundation before we prescribe your plan. Think of it as the prerequisite course.

What to Expect

You may or may not see the scale move — and that's completely normal. Some clients see early progress just from tracking. Others need a few weeks to calibrate. Either way, this phase is working.

What We're Watching

  • Your real-life calorie baseline
  • Typical daily steps & water
  • Tracking consistency & habits
  • Your lifestyle patterns & schedule

Graduate to Stage 2 When...

  • Logging food 80–90% of days
  • Baseline steps & water established
  • Coach can read your maintenance calories
  • Communication rhythm is working

Weeks 4–6
The Prescription
Your Custom Plan Begins
▼

What's Happening

You've earned it. This is where your coach lays out your full game plan — your deficit, your workouts, your specific goals. We'll also talk through your history and get ahead of the places people typically fall off.

What to Expect

Things get more intentional here. You'll have specific targets to hit. Progress should start to become visible. This is the beginning of your 12–16 week fat loss window.

What's Being Prescribed

  • Your calorie or step-based deficit
  • Workout program (introduced gradually)
  • Daily step & hydration targets
  • Your non-negotiables & habit plan

What to Master

  • Consistently hitting your nutrition targets
  • Getting to the gym & learning your workouts
  • Understanding & owning your plan

Weeks 7–12
The Work
Active Fat Loss + Progressive Overload
▼

Weeks 7–10: Momentum

You're in it. This is where consistency builds real results. We'll also anticipate the most common slip point — when the novelty wears off and urgency drops — and make sure you push through it.

Weeks 10–12: Optimization

Locking in the habits that will stick forever. We'll review what's genuinely changed, what still needs work, and start having the bigger picture conversation about what comes next.

What May Be Added

  • Cardio (only if progress stalls & food is already low)
  • Nutrient timing around workouts
  • Light food quality coaching
  • Step increases before calorie cuts

What to Master

  • Training 3–4 days/week consistently
  • Tracking without overthinking it
  • Progressive overload in the gym
  • Knowing your personal obstacles & how to manage them

Weeks 12–13
The Reassessment
Milestone Check-In + Your Goal Path Call
▼

What's Happening

Time to zoom out. Where did you start? Where are you now? Where do you still want to go? Your coach will review your full progress — physical, habit, and mindset — and map out what comes next.

Two Paths Forward

Close to goal: Begin a 3–6 week reverse/maintenance phase, then transition to Legacy or continued support.

More work to do: This is your Phase II conversation — options, timeline, and what the continued journey looks like.

What We Review Together

  • Physical progress: scale, measurements, how you feel
  • Habit shifts: what has genuinely changed for good vs. what's still inconsistent
  • Mindset growth: which limiting beliefs have shifted
  • Remaining goals and realistic timeline to get there

Weeks 13–16
The Next Chapter
Phase II or Legacy — Your Continued Journey
▼

This Isn't Starting Over

You have all the skills now. Phase II is where we use everything you've built — and finish the job. Most clients reach their full goal in 10–12 months total. That's not failure. That's the process working exactly as designed.

About the Reverse Phase

After a long cut, your metabolism needs to reset. A controlled 3–6 week reverse brings your calories back up, restores energy, improves gym performance, and sets your body up to respond faster and more efficiently in the next cut.

Phase II Options

  • 4 months — Close to goal; mainly reverse + maintenance
  • 6 months — Most common; moderate remaining work
  • 8 months — Meaningful goals still ahead
  • 12 months — Comprehensive transformation

Legacy Program

When you've reached your goal and are ready to maintain independently, Legacy gives you continued access to workouts, weekly group calls, and check-ins — without a dedicated 1:1 coach. It's your forever home.
